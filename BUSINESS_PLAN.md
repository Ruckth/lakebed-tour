# Business Plan: Immersive Direct Booking Platform for Luxury Boutique Resorts

## 1. Executive Summary

We build premium direct booking websites for luxury boutique resorts in Southeast Asia. Our platform combines immersive 360-degree virtual tours, AI-powered concierge chatbots, and conversion-optimized booking flows to help resort owners reduce OTA dependency and increase direct revenue.

**The problem:** Boutique resort owners lose 15-25% of every booking to OTA commissions (Booking.com, Airbnb, Agoda) — often $100,000-$175,000+ per year. Most have poor or outdated websites that cannot compete with OTA user experience.

**The solution:** A turnkey, custom-branded direct booking website that converts browsers into bookers through immersive 360-degree tours (67% higher booking likelihood), AI concierge chat (35% increase in direct bookings), and transparent OTA price comparison that makes the savings undeniable.

**Revenue model:** $5,000-$8,000 setup fee + $300-$500/month recurring. Target 20 clients in Year 1, scaling to 80+ by Year 3.

## 3. Product

### 3.1 Core Features (Included in Setup)

| Feature | Impact | Data Point |
|---------|--------|------------|
| **360-Degree Virtual Tours** | Immersive room-by-room exploration with hotspot navigation | 67% higher booking likelihood (Omni Hotels study) |
| **Narrative Storytelling** | Emotional overlays during tours — headlines, vignettes, "imagine yourself here" moments | 48% increase in direct bookings for properties with virtual tours |

- **Net savings Year 2+:** $15,896/year
- **ROI:** 133% Year 1, 331% Year 2+

Even shifting just **5 bookings per month** from OTA to direct covers the entire platform cost.

## 6. Go-to-Market Strategy

### 6.1 Phase 1: Founder-Led Sales (Month 1-6)

**Target:** 5-10 clients in Koh Samui + Bali

**Channels:**
- **Direct outreach** — LinkedIn, email to resort owners/GMs found via Google Maps, TripAdvisor, Booking.com listings
- **Demo-first approach** — Send personalized demo URL with prospect's resort name before the call
- **WhatsApp-native sales** — SEA business communication happens on WhatsApp
- **Referral incentive** — 1 month free for successful referral
- **Hospitality meetups** — Thailand Hotel & Travel Tech Conference, Bali hospitality networking events

**Sales process:**
1. Identify prospect on OTA listings (look for "Superhost" or 4.5+ rated, 5-30 rooms)
2. Customize demo with their resort name and details (takes 5 minutes)
3. Send demo link via WhatsApp/email: "We built a preview of what your direct booking site could look like"
4. Video call demo walkthrough (15 minutes)
5. Close with ROI calculator showing their specific commission savings

### 6.2 Phase 2: Inbound + Partnerships (Month 6-12)

- **Content marketing** — Blog posts: "How much is Booking.com really costing your resort?", "The direct booking playbook for boutique resorts"
- **SEO** — Target "direct booking website for resorts", "resort website builder"
- **Partnerships** — 360 photography companies in Bali/Thailand, hospitality consultants, interior designers
- **Case studies** — Document client results (before/after OTA dependency, revenue impact)

### 6.3 Phase 3: Scale (Year 2+)

- Expand to Vietnam, Philippines, Sri Lanka, Maldives
- Hire local sales reps in each market
- Build admin dashboard for self-service property management
- Explore white-label partnership with tourism boards
